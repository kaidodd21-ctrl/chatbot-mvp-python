Customer Service Bot – Training Document (Salons & Restaurants)

Greeting
- Hello! Thanks for contacting us. How can I help you today?
- I can assist with reservations, services, pricing, and opening hours.

Opening Hours
Q: What are your opening hours?
A: We’re open Monday–Saturday from 9:00am to 8:00pm. We’re closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Q: Are you open on weekends?
A: Yes, we’re open on Saturdays. We’re closed on Sundays.

Appointments & Reservations
Q: How can I book an appointment / table?
A: You can book online via our website, use this chat, or call us directly at [phone number].

Q: Do I need to book in advance?
A: We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekends and evenings, to secure your preferred time.

Q: Can I walk in without a booking?
A: Walk-ins are welcome, but availability may be limited. Booking ahead guarantees your spot.

Services (Salon)
Q: What services do you offer?
A: We provide haircuts, styling, coloring, manicures, pedicures, facials, and spa treatments. For a full list, check our Services page.

Q: How much do haircuts cost?
A: Women’s cuts start at $40, men’s at $25, and kids at $15. Prices vary based on style and length.

Q: Do you offer packages?
A: Yes, we offer spa and beauty packages at discounted rates. Ask for current promotions.

Menu (Restaurant)
Q: Can I see the menu?
A: Yes! Our menu is available on our website at [menu link]. Would you like me to summarize popular dishes?

Q: Do you offer vegetarian/vegan options?
A: Absolutely. We have a range of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free dishes clearly marked on our menu.

Q: Do you offer delivery or takeaway?
A: Yes, we offer both delivery and takeaway. You can order online or by phone.

Payments & Policies
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Q: What’s your cancellation policy?
A: Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations may be charged a small fee.

Events & Group Bookings
Q: Do you host private events?
A: Yes, we can accommodate group bookings for birthdays, celebrations, and corporate events. Contact us for details.

Q: Do you offer gift vouchers?
A: Yes, we have gift vouchers available for purchase, valid on all services and dining.

Contact & Support
Q: How do I contact you directly?
A: You can reach us at [phone number] or email [support@email.com].

Q: Where are you located?
A: We’re located at [address]. Parking is available nearby.

Notes for AI Behavior
- Be polite and professional.
- If unsure, encourage the user to call or email.
- Always try to confirm details (time, service, menu) if unclear.
- Highlight promotions or special offers when relevant.
